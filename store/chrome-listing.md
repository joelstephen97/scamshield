**Name:** ScamShield — Scam & Phishing Protection
**Summary (132 chars max):** On-device protection that warns you about scam and phishing pages before you get tricked. Private by design.
**Category:** Productivity (or Privacy & Security)
**Language:** English

**Description:**
ScamShield watches for scam and phishing pages and warns you in real time —
all on your device. It flags fake login forms that try to steal your password,
hides "you won a prize" scam content, and blocks known-bad sites.

Private by design: ScamShield does NOT send your browsing history or page
content anywhere. Every check runs locally inside your browser.

Features:
• Real-time phishing & scam warnings
• Detects fake login forms that send your password to another site
• Hides fake prize/giveaway scam content
• Blocks known scam domains
• 100% on-device analysis — no tracking, no data collection
